ShiftSnap

Empowering hourly workers with seamless shift management and flexible scheduling.

Lead Product Designer12 weeksiOS Mobile App
Project overview

A mobile-first workforce management experience.

ShiftSnap helps hourly workers view schedules, trade shifts, request coverage, and communicate with managers without the friction of legacy desktop-first tools.

The product balances employee autonomy with the oversight managers need to maintain reliable coverage.

The problem

Hourly workers struggle with inflexible scheduling and fragmented communication.

3–5 days

45%

Key insights

Flexibility only works when communication and context are clear.

01

Workers need autonomy

Last-minute flexibility matters, but requesting help often creates guilt or uncertainty.

02

Managers lose time

Scheduling logistics consume 8–12 hours each week.

03

Communication breaks

Most scheduling errors begin with unclear or missed updates.

Solution

A focused mobile system for schedules, swaps, and coverage.

01

Schedule at a glance

Clear weekly shifts, changes, and availability in one mobile view.

02

Request, review, and confirm swaps without long message threads.

03

04

Manager approvals

Context-rich decisions with coverage and overtime implications.

Feature 01

Smart Shift Swapping

Workers can initiate swap requests directly from their schedule. The system automatically matches qualified coworkers and keeps everyone informed throughout the process.

  • One-tap swap initiation from any shift
  • Filtered coworker list by qualifications
