Project overview

Reframing a feature-heavy banking app around the moments that matter most.

The redesign explores a simpler way for UBA customers to manage money across accounts, complete high-frequency tasks, and understand every transaction before committing.

My roleLead Product Designer

Research synthesis, UX strategy, interaction design, prototyping, and design-system direction.

FocusTrust + task speed

Transfers, bill payment, card controls, transaction clarity, and low-connectivity resilience.

Outcome targetsLess friction, fewer errors

Improve task completion and confidence without sacrificing security.

01 · The challenge

Banking should feel empowering, not operational.

Customers could complete core tasks, but dense navigation, technical language, and delayed reassurance made routine actions feel riskier than necessary. The redesign focused on reducing decision friction at every critical moment.

Research synthesis

Three behaviors shaped the experience strategy.

01

People scan before they act

Balances, recent activity, and pending items need to be understandable within seconds.

02

Trust is built before confirmation

Recipient verification, fees, and arrival timing must appear before a transfer is submitted.

03

Everyday tasks need shortcuts

Airtime, bills, and repeat transfers should require fewer steps without hiding important context.

02 · Experience principles

Fast when familiar. Explicit when consequential.

01

Clarity before action

Explain the recipient, cost, timing, and consequence before a customer commits.

02

Progress without ambiguity

Every financial action communicates processing, success, or recovery in plain language.

03

Control close at hand

Frequent actions and card safeguards remain immediately accessible.

04

Resilient by design

Save progress, communicate connectivity, and prevent accidental duplicate transactions.

Information architecture

Core journeys organized around intent, not product terminology.

Home→Review money→Choose action→Verify details→Confirm securely→Track outcome
