01 · Project overview

A connected learning system for focus, belonging, and measurable progress.

Remote programs often fail because the learning experience fragments across video calls, slide decks, chat threads, LMS modules, and private notes. Loop Learning brings those moments into one coherent workflow: live learning, practice, reflection, feedback, and instructor follow-up.

Outcome focus

Reduce disengagement

02 · The challenge

Remote learning had content, but not enough human signal.

Students were completing tasks, yet instructors could not tell who was confident, who was stuck, and who was quietly drifting away. The product problem was not more content. It was designing a system that made support visible, timely, and actionable.

Disconnected tools

Live sessions, homework, peer critique, and instructor feedback lived in separate places, making progress hard to understand.

Invisible confusion

Students often waited too long to ask for help because the platform treated silence as progress.

Instructor overload

Program teams were forced to scan comments, attendance, and assignment status manually before taking action.

Research synthesis

Three needs shaped the product direction.

The strongest insight was simple: learners do not just need access to information. They need a visible loop of practice, feedback, confidence, and next steps.

01

Presence before content

Students were more likely to return when the platform showed who else was learning, asking, reviewing, and progressing.

02

Progress without anxiety

Progress tracking needed to feel orienting, not punitive. Learners wanted a clear path without feeling watched.

03

Support at the right moment

Instructors needed signals that separated normal struggle from disengagement risk.

Critical workflow

From joining a cohort to receiving useful feedback.

Loop was designed around one repeatable learning rhythm: orient, participate, practice, get feedback, reflect, and continue.

01

Orient

Student sees today's objective, prep status, and cohort activity.

02

Participate

Live studio keeps session materials, chat, prompts, and questions together.

03

Practice

Assignments break complex work into guided checkpoints.

04

Feedback

Peer and instructor critique becomes structured and actionable.

05

Reflect

AI coach summarizes gaps, next steps, and confidence patterns.
